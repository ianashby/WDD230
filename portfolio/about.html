Hello, I'm Ian,

I'm a photographer based in the Southeast Idaho Area. I started taking pictures at a very young age when I would go on fishing trips with my dad. From there, I took photography classes in High School and started to learn a lot about techniques and foundations in art.

I started doing profession photography the summer before my senior year where I photographed many of my friends and classmates for their senior photos. After that summer, everything started to take off! I have now been a part of many shoots for weddings, engagements, seniors, and families.

I enjoy photography for the many adventures it grants me. I have traveled to many different places that I have loved.
